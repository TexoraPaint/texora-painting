Full Drywall Installation Services in Port Moody

Port Moody is experiencing a wave of basement suite development and whole-home renovations as homeowners maximize the value of their properties near Burrard Inlet. Whether you're developing a legal basement suite in a Glenayre split-level, finishing a bonus room in a Heritage Mountain home, or gut-renovating a craftsman property in Pleasantside, the quality of your drywall installation sets the foundation for every finish that follows. Uneven boards, poorly taped joints, and inadequate finishing all show up under paint — especially under the raking natural light that floods through the large windows characteristic of Port Moody's Westcoast Modern homes.

Texora Painting installs drywall to a Level 4 finish as standard on all painted wall surfaces — the level required for smooth and eggshell-painted walls where any shadowing from joints would be visible. For ceilings or accent walls with very flat paint sheens or dramatic lighting conditions, we can apply a Level 5 skim coat finish that eliminates all surface texture variation entirely. We use moisture-resistant board (green board or purple board) in all below-grade spaces, bathrooms, and laundry areas — critical in Port Moody where below-grade moisture is a known issue in many neighbourhoods near the water table or hillside drainage paths.

We handle the complete scope: supply, hang, tape, mud, sand, and deliver walls that are primed and ready for your painter. Or, since we're a full-service painting contractor, we can take the project all the way to finished painted walls with a single contract and single crew — saving you the coordination and schedule risk of managing separate trades. Most single-room installations in Port Moody can be completed in two to three days including curing time for joint compound.

Our Drywall Installation Process

1

On-Site Quote

We measure the space, confirm board type (standard, moisture-resistant, or fire-rated), assess ceiling height and complexity, and provide a fixed written quote covering supply, hang, and finish.

2

Board Supply & Hang

We supply and hang drywall panels efficiently, cutting around openings and electrical boxes cleanly, and fastening to code with the correct screw pattern and spacing throughout.

3

Tape, Mud & Sand

All joints are taped with fibreglass mesh and coated with three coats of joint compound — each allowed to cure fully before sanding — delivering a Level 4 flat, seamless finish.

4

Prime-Ready Delivery

The finished walls are ready for primer and paint. We can prime and paint as part of the same project, or hand off to your preferred painter with walls in perfect condition.
