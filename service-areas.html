About Us

Painting with Pride
Since Day One

Texora Painting is a family-run residential painting company based in Tsawwassen, BC. We transform homes across Tsawwassen, Ladner & Delta — one room, one house, one neighbourhood at a time.

We started small, with a simple belief: do excellent work, treat every homeowner with respect, and never cut corners. That mindset earned us 5-star reviews, repeat customers, and referrals that keep our calendar full.

Every project — whether it's a single room refresh or a full exterior repaint — gets the same care and attention. We show up on time, clean up after ourselves, and don't leave until you're happy.

Meet the Team

Ruslan

Owner

"I started Texora Painting because I believe every homeowner deserves honest, quality work — the kind you'd be proud to do on your own home."

Growing up with a passion for craftsmanship, I built Texora Painting from the ground up with one simple goal: show up, do the job right, and leave every client genuinely happy. What drives me every single day is the look on a homeowner's face when they see their space transformed.

Every project gets my personal attention because, at the end of the day, we're working in people's homes — and that means everything to me.

When I'm not on the job, you'll find me exploring BC's beautiful outdoors with my wife and our dog — hiking trails, road-tripping along the coast, and discovering new corners of this incredible province.
