Port Moody's Trusted Interior Painting Professionals

Port Moody is one of the most architecturally diverse communities in Metro Vancouver. Along the shores of Burrard Inlet and climbing into the forested slopes of Heritage Mountain, you'll find everything from century-old Edwardian and craftsman bungalows in College Park and Pleasantside to sleek Westcoast Modern homes with floor-to-ceiling windows in Old Orchard and Glenayre. Painting these homes well requires more than a steady hand — it requires understanding how each style of construction performs in Port Moody's cool, heavily forested microclimate where moisture is a constant consideration.

Texora Painting has experience working across all of Port Moody's neighbourhoods, from the boutique strata units of Newport Village to the larger single-family homes near Rocky Point Park. We assess every surface before we paint: older craftsman homes often have horsehair plaster walls or oil-based layers that need proper adhesion primers, while newer builds require low-sheen finishes that complement the mountain views without washing out natural light. Every project gets a tailored prep and paint plan — not a one-size-fits-all approach.

Our crews protect your home as if it were our own. Furniture is moved and covered, floors are laid with professional drop cloths, and we seal off painting areas to contain dust and fumes. We use premium Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore products in low-VOC formulations, which is especially important in Port Moody strata buildings where hallway air is shared. When we finish, we walk through every room with you and address any touch-ups on the spot before packing up.

Our Interior Painting Process

1

Free In-Home Quote

We visit your Port Moody home, assess existing surfaces — including older plaster or heritage trim — discuss colour options, and provide a firm written quote.

2

Prep & Protect

All surfaces are cleaned, holes filled, sanding completed, and primer applied. Floors, furniture, and fixtures are fully protected before a brush touches the wall.

3

Expert Application

Two full coats applied with professional rollers and brushes. We maintain wet edges for a uniform finish and cut precise lines at every ceiling, baseboard, and door casing.

4

Final Walkthrough

We inspect every room with you in natural light, touch up on the spot, and don't leave until the result meets the standard you were quoted.
