Complete Home Repaints Across Metro Vancouver

A full home repaint is the single most transformative thing you can do to a property — interior, exterior, or both. Whether you're preparing a home for sale and need to maximize first impressions, moving into a new place and want to make it yours, or simply refreshing a home that hasn't been painted in a decade, Texora Painting delivers a cohesive, professional result from start to finish.

We handle complete repaints under one quote and one crew — walls, ceilings, trim, baseboards, doors, and closets. No coordinating between contractors, no mismatched timelines, no inconsistency between rooms. We serve homeowners throughout Metro Vancouver including Vancouver, Burnaby, Coquitlam, Delta, Tsawwassen, Ladner, North Vancouver, Surrey, and Richmond.

A full repaint is also the most efficient way to address accumulated wear across your home — dents, scuffs, colour inconsistencies from patching, yellowed trim, and dated colour palettes. Done in one coordinated project, the whole home looks intentional and refreshed rather than piecemeal.

What's Included in a Full Home Repaint

One Quote Covers Everything

We assess the entire home and provide a single all-in quote covering prep, materials, and labour. No scope creep, no surprises. If we find additional work (drywall repairs, extra coats on dark walls), we tell you upfront.

Colour Consultation

Choosing a whole-home palette can be overwhelming. We'll walk through your home with you, discuss your goals and preferences, and suggest colour schemes that flow naturally from room to room and work with your lighting conditions.

Interior Full Repaint Scope

Typically includes all walls, all ceilings, all trim and baseboards, all doors and casings, closet interiors, and staircase walls. We can include or exclude any scope based on your needs.

Pre-Sale Repaints

We work with homeowners and realtors preparing homes for sale. A fresh paint job is consistently cited as one of the highest-ROI pre-sale improvements — we can prioritize key rooms, work around showings, and move quickly when timelines are tight.

Our Painting Process — Step by Step

1

Free On-Site Quote

We visit your home, assess the scope, and provide a detailed written quote — no surprise charges. Most quotes are delivered same or next day.

2

Colour Consultation

Not sure on colours? We'll bring swatches, walk you through finishes, and help you choose a palette that works with your lighting and furnishings.

3

Surface Preparation

This is the most important step. We patch, sand, fill holes, tape, and prime every surface. Proper prep is what separates a lasting finish from one that chips and peels.

4

Protection of Your Home

Floors, furniture, and fixtures are fully covered before we open a single can. We treat your home with the same care we'd want in our own.

5

Application

We apply paint using the right method for each surface — brush, roller, or spray — to achieve an even, professional finish every time.

6

Cleanup & Walkthrough

We leave your home cleaner than we found it. Once complete, we walk through the job with you to make sure you're 100% satisfied before we pack up.

7

Follow-Up

We stand behind our work. If anything needs touching up after we leave, call us and we'll be back — no questions asked.
