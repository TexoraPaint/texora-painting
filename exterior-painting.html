Professional Exterior Painting in Metro Vancouver

Exterior painting in Metro Vancouver is not just cosmetic — it's one of the most important layers of protection your home has against BC's relentless rain, humidity, UV, and freeze-thaw cycles. The wrong paint, applied to a poorly prepped surface, fails within 2–3 years. A properly prepared and coated exterior, done right, should last 8–12 years. We make sure you're in that second category.

Texora Painting serves homeowners across Metro Vancouver including Vancouver, Burnaby, Coquitlam, Delta, Tsawwassen, Ladner, North Vancouver, Surrey, Richmond, and New Westminster. Our crews are experienced with the full range of exterior materials found in BC homes — wood siding, Hardie board, stucco, vinyl siding, cedar, and more.

We monitor weather windows carefully and never start an exterior job when rain is in the short-term forecast. Paint needs specific temperature and humidity conditions to bond and cure correctly — we've learned what works in the Lower Mainland through years of experience, not guesswork.

What's Included in Every Exterior Paint Job

Why Prep Makes or Breaks Exterior Paint

We pressure wash every surface, scrape and sand loose paint, fill cracks and gaps with exterior-grade caulk, spot-prime bare wood, and fully mask windows and fixtures before opening a single can of paint. This is the step that separates a 3-year paint job from a 10-year one.

What We Paint on Your Exterior

House siding (wood, Hardie board, stucco, vinyl), trim, soffits, fascia, eaves, doors, garages, fences, decks, pergolas, and outbuildings. If it's on the outside of your property, we make it look great and protect it properly.

Exterior Coatings for BC's Climate

We use elastomeric, 100% acrylic, and alkyd exterior coatings selected for durability in Metro Vancouver's wet winters and dry summers. We recommend the right product for your siding type, substrate, and sun exposure.

Project Timeline

A typical house exterior takes 3–6 days depending on size, condition, and weather windows. We plan around forecasts to ensure paint cures properly and never rush a job at the expense of quality.
